About Me

Engineering approach

I work on sensor-driven control and measurement systems where system behavior is often limited by measurement quality rather than control algorithms.

  • Validate sensor behavior before controller tuning
  • Explicitly analyze noise, bias, and quantization effects
  • Test assumptions using real hardware, not only simulations
  • Design systems to remain predictable under non-ideal conditions
